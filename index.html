Manuel Salvadores   PhD  (msalvadores AT gmail DOT com)
There is so much data out there that is providing such little value to our health. I co-founded Lexigram to take medical data – from EHRs, doctor's notes, email, faxes, claims, and more – and produce intelligent summaries for doctors, software engineers, and data scientists.
I recently co-founded Lexigram with Paul Alexander to help data scientists and clinicians work with large sets of patient data, both structure and unstructured data.

I was a Senior Research Engineer at Stanford University and I still occasionally consult for the BioPortal project and the Stanford Center for Biomedical Informatics Research part of the School of Medicine. Before coming to the US I lived in the UK where I was a Research Fellow at the School of Electronics and Computer Science at Southampton University. I have 15 years of experience in Software Engineering, both research and industry, and I obtained a PhD and BS in Computer Sciences from the Polytechnic University of Madrid.
